Government Websites Related to Agriculture

# Website Description
1 Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare The official website of the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, India, providing information about policies, schemes, and programs related to agriculture and farmers.
2 Indian Council of Agricultural Research Focuses on agricultural research and education. It aims to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability through research and innovation.
3 Indian Agricultural Research Institute A leading agricultural research institute in India, focusing on research and development in agriculture and allied sciences.
4 Agricultural Portal A comprehensive portal providing information on various agricultural schemes, subsidies, and resources available to farmers.
5 National Horticulture Board Provides information on horticultural research, development, and schemes. It supports the development of the horticulture sector in India.
6 Krishi Kosh A digital repository of agricultural research and extension literature. It aims to make research outputs accessible to the agricultural community.
7 National Cooperative Development Corporation Offers support for cooperative development in agriculture and rural development. It aims to promote and support agricultural cooperatives.
8 Department of Agriculture and Marketing Provides details about agricultural marketing, development schemes, and support programs for farmers.
9 SeedNet India A platform for the seed sector providing information on seed production, certification, and quality standards.
10 Central Soil and Water Conservation Research and Training Institute Focuses on research and training related to soil and water conservation, aiming to improve agricultural productivity and sustainability.
11 Agri Business and Agro Tourism Corporation Provides information on agri-business opportunities, agro-tourism projects, and agricultural development initiatives.
12 National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development Supports agriculture and rural development through financial assistance, research, and policy interventions.
13 National Project on Farming Communities Aims to support farming communities through various projects and schemes, enhancing agricultural practices and productivity.
14 Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority Focuses on the promotion of agricultural and processed food exports from India, providing support and resources for exporters.

16 Rural Development Ministry Focuses on rural development projects, including agricultural development, poverty alleviation, and infrastructure improvement.
17 Green Earth Foundation Supports sustainable agricultural practices and environmental conservation through various programs and initiatives.
18 Agri Informatics Provides information and resources related to agricultural informatics, including data analysis and technology in agriculture.

23 SABMiller Agricultural Development Provides resources and support for agricultural development, focusing on improving productivity and sustainability.
24 National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) - Agriculture Offers payment solutions and financial services to support agricultural transactions and development.
25 United Nations - Food Security Provides global information and initiatives related to food security and agricultural development.
26 Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) Offers global information, research, and support for sustainable agriculture and food security.
27 Center for International Forestry Research (CIFOR) Focuses on research related to forestry and agriculture, promoting sustainable land use practices.
28 International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) Provides funding and support for agricultural development projects in developing countries.
